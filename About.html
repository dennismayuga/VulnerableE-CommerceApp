ABOUT US

Business history :

Our website was founded in 2010 by a group of friends who were passionate about sustainability and the reuse of goods. At first, our website started as a small-scale platform where people could buy and sell used items in their local area. However, as our reputation grew and more people joined our community, our website expanded to cover the entire country. Over the years, we have facilitated the sale of thousands of used items, ranging from clothing and electronics to furniture and automobiles. Our website provides a safe and reliable platform for people to sell their belongings and make extra money, while also helping to reduce waste and promote sustainability. We pride ourselves on our excellent customer service and user-friendly interface, which make it easy for people to browse and purchase items on our website. We are constantly striving to improve our services and stay up-to-date with the latest technology and trends in the second-hand goods industry.
